Basic Exercise Principles

Introduction

For those interested in exercise science and how certain exercises affect you, the Prepilin's Chart will be essential for power and olympic lifting purposes. Those aiming for an increased personal record for lifts invlolving the typical bench, squat, deadlift, cling and press etc. will have to abide by a reasonable program. Details will be explained in the next paragraph.

Details Explained

Different rep ranges involve different purposes when building a program based off of the Prepilin's Chart. Muscle builidng (8-15 reps), strength (5-7), and power (1-4) all play important roles while using the Prepilin's Chart. All need to be present in a program for an appropiate duration to allow the human body to recuperate properly.
